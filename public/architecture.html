Architecture

This page documents the full JLT Platform Engineering model — from identity and access control, to platform services, observability, runbooks, and deployment guardrails. The diagrams below should be read from top to bottom as one connected platform lifecycle.

Diagrams + design decisions for the JLT-Lane ecosystem.

Key design principles

  • Modularity: independently deployable components.
  • Security by design: least privilege + encrypted stores.
  • Observability: logs, metrics, tracing everywhere.
  • Automation: IaC + CI/CD for repeatability.
