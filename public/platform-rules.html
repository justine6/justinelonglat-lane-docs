Design perspective

  • Systems over components: every part is designed in relation to the whole.
  • Visibility is foundational: metrics, logs, and traces are part of the architecture, not added later.
  • Operate what you design: runbooks and recovery paths are built alongside the system.
  • Learn through iteration: sandbox environments allow continuous validation and improvement.
