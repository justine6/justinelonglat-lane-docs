Platform Runbooks

Operational playbooks for the JLT-Lane engineering platform. These runbooks describe how to start, verify, troubleshoot, and recover the sandbox observability stack and related platform services.

Why these runbooks exist

Architecture explains how the platform is designed. Runbooks explain how the platform is operated in real conditions. Together, they form the bridge between documentation and execution.

These pages are intended to support repeatable operations across Docker-based local environments, Prometheus metric collection, Grafana dashboards, and service recovery workflows in the JLT-Lane sandbox suite.

Operating model

These runbooks follow a common troubleshooting pattern: 

Observe symptom
        ↓
Verify service/container state
        ↓
Check metrics or targets
        ↓
Confirm configuration
        ↓
Restart / recover safely
        ↓
Validate platform behavior

This keeps recovery steps explainable, repeatable, and safe across environments.
